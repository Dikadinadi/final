Im Hye-kyung (Korean: 임혜경, born August 19, 1991)



Cheeze

CHEEZE was originally a 4-piece band, but after the line-up changes, our beautiful vocalist Dalchon was the only member left. The artist decided to adopt CHEEZE as her pseudonym when she began her solo career as a singer and songwriter. Since then, CHEEZE has released many significant drama soundtracks and collaborated with big k-pop artists like Eric Nam and pH-1. Show off to your friends that you knew a talented singer like Dalchon before she became world famous.
